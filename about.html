Our Story

Beekeepers First, Bee Removers Second

Bee Green Natural Bee Removal was founded on one simple belief: bees are far too important to kill.

What began as a couple of beekeepers helping neighbors with backyard swarms has grown into a full live-removal service across California and Tennessee. Every colony we pull out of a wall, tree, or roof is handed off to a local beekeeper partner — never exterminated.

Our technicians pair real beekeeping knowledge with hands-on construction experience, so we can open up a structure, remove every bit of comb, and seal it back up properly. The bees get a healthy second home, and your property is protected from the rot, stains, and pests an abandoned hive leaves behind.

We answer the phone 24/7, give free written estimates, and back our work with a warranty — because doing right by the bees and by our customers is the entire point.
