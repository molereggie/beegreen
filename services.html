What We Do

Our Bee & Stinging Pest Services

Every job is handled by a trained, fully-equipped technician — with a written warranty and a focus on protecting pollinators wherever we can.

Most Common

Live Bee Swarm Removal

Active swarms on your home, fence, tree, or vehicle captured and relocated safely. Swarms move fast and become permanent hives — we respond quickly.

  • Live capture & relocation
  • Same-day emergency response

Beehive Extraction

Full removal of established hives from inside walls, attics, roofs, and trees. We extract all honeycomb to prevent future infestations and structural damage.

  • Complete comb & honey removal
  • Structural cleanup & sealing

Hazardous

Underground Yellow Jacket Removal

Dangerous subterranean yellow jacket nests removed safely with full protective equipment. One of the most hazardous jobs in pest removal — don't DIY this one.

  • Full protective treatment
  • Nest located & eliminated
Request service

Wasp Nest Removal

Professional elimination of wasp nests from eaves, porch ceilings, attics, and exterior walls. We remove the nest entirely and treat the area to prevent return.

  • Eaves, attics & exterior walls
  • Preventative area treatment
Request service

Mud Dauber Nest Removal

Removal of mud dauber and other solitary-wasp nests from walls, eaves, sheds, and equipment — cleaned off and treated so they don't rebuild in the same spot.

  • Nest removal & surface cleanup
  • Re-nesting prevention
Request service

Bee-Proofing

After removal, we seal entry points and bee-proof vulnerable spots — vents, gaps, eaves, and cavities — so a new colony can't move into the same place again.
