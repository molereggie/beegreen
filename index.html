Our Services

Complete Bee & Stinging Pest Removal

From a single swarm to a colony deep inside your walls — we handle every type of bee and stinging pest, humanely and professionally.

Live Bee Swarm Removal

Active swarms on your home, fence, tree, or vehicle captured and relocated safely. We respond fast — swarms move quickly and become hives.

Beehive Extraction

Full removal of established beehives from inside walls, attics, roofs, and trees. We extract all honeycomb to prevent future infestations and structural damage.

Underground Yellow Jacket Removal

Dangerous subterranean yellow jacket nests removed safely with full protective equipment. One of the most hazardous jobs in pest removal — don't DIY this one.

Wasp Nest Removal

Professional elimination of wasp nests from eaves, porch ceilings, attics, and exterior walls. We remove the nest entirely and treat the area to prevent return.

Mud Dauber Nest Removal

Mud daubers build tube nests on walls, ceilings, and siding. We remove them cleanly, clean the surface, and recommend preventive sealing.
